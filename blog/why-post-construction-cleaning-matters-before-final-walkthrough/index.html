Why Post-Construction Cleaning Matters Before Final Walkthrough

Use this checklist-focused guide to plan post-construction cleanup before walkthrough and occupancy milestones.

Why standard cleaning is not enough

Post-construction conditions include fine dust, adhesive residue, and edge buildup that routine janitorial cleaning is not designed to handle.

What final-phase cleanup should address

  • Fine dust removal: Vents, ledges, door tops, and horizontal surfaces.
  • Glass and reflective surfaces: Smudges, film, and residue.
  • Floor detail work: Corners, edges, and transition lines.
  • Fixture wipe-downs: Switch plates, trim, and hardware surfaces.

Next step

Provide project phase, walkthrough date, and access constraints so final cleanup can be scheduled at the right time.
