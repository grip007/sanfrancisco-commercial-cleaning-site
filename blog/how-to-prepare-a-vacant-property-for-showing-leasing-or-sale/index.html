How to Prepare a Vacant Property for Showing, Leasing, or Sale

Use this framework to prioritize cleaning tasks based on whether the property is being shown, leased, or listed for sale.

Vacant properties need presentation planning

A vacant space can highlight every flaw. Cleaning should be scoped to improve perceived condition and reduce distractions during tours.

Showing-ready vs leasing-ready vs sale-ready

  • Showing-ready: Fast visual reset for tours and photos.
  • Leasing-ready: Deeper detail where applicants notice condition quality.
  • Sale-ready: Broader cleanup that supports inspections and listing presentation.

Next step

Include your target listing or showing date so cleaning can be timed around photos, tours, and handoff activities.
