Commercial Cleaning vs. Janitorial Services: What’s the Difference?

Use this comparison to choose the right mix of recurring janitorial support and project-based commercial cleaning.

The short answer

Janitorial service usually refers to recurring maintenance tasks. Commercial cleaning often includes broader, project-based, or specialized scopes beyond routine upkeep.

What janitorial service usually covers

  • Scheduled restroom cleaning and restocking-ready wipe-downs
  • Trash removal and liner replacement
  • Breakroom and shared-surface reset
  • Routine floor and touchpoint care

What commercial cleaning may include

  • Initial deep cleaning before a recurring plan starts
  • Move-out, turnover, and handoff cleaning
  • Post-construction cleanup phases
  • Exterior presentation tasks such as power washing support
