What Property Managers Should Expect From a Turnover Cleaning Crew

This overview helps property managers set clear turnover expectations before showing, leasing, or handoff deadlines.

Turnover cleaning should be timeline-aware

A good turnover cleaning scope supports leasing speed, showing readiness, and handoff deadlines.

Pre-lease / pre-showing checklist logic

  • Kitchen and bath reset: Surfaces and fixtures addressed for presentation.
  • Floors and edges: Corners, baseboard lines, and entry thresholds cleaned.
  • Dust and debris: Cabinets, sills, and overlooked ledges cleared.
  • Odor and trash removal: Remaining waste and obvious odor sources addressed.

Next step

Include vacancy status, target showing date, and condition notes so the turnover scope aligns with your timeline.
